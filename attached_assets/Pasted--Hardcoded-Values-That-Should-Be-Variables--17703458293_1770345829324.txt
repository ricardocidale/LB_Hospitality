  Already Done Right

  The codebase handles most values well — these are all properly extracted to constants or configurable via the UI:
  - All expense rates (costRateRooms, costRateFB, etc.)
  - Depreciation period (27.5 years)
  - All debt/financing parameters (LTV, interest rates, terms)
  - Commission rates, management fees, catering boosts
  - Inflation rate, tax rates, exit cap rates

  The biggest gaps are #1 (10-year projection locked everywhere), #2 (staffing model completely hardcoded), and #3 (existing constant ignored).
