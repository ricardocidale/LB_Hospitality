# Skill: Marketing Costs Research

## Overview
Research hospitality-specific marketing costs, digital spend, OTA commissions, and direct booking costs for a specific property type and location.

## Key Metrics to Extract
- **marketingCostRate**: Annual marketing spend as a percentage of total property revenue.
- **otaCommissions**: Average commission rates for Booking.com, Expedia, and local OTAs.
- **directBookingCost**: Typical cost per conversion for direct hotel bookings.

## Research Strategy
1. Search for hospitality marketing benchmark reports (e.g., Kalibri Labs, HVS).
2. Look for regional OTA commission trends.
3. Check digital marketing spend averages for boutique and luxury segments.

## Output Format
```json
{
  "marketingCosts": {
    "marketingCostRate": "3.5% - 5.0% of total revenue",
    "otaCommissions": "15% - 22%",
    "directBookingCost": "$15 - $25 per night",
    "sources": ["Kalibri Labs", "HVS Marketing Benchmarks"]
  }
}
```
