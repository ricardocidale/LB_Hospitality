  C1. Net Income Omits Depreciation & Taxes (Monthly Engine)

  financialEngine.ts:401
  netIncome = noi - interestExpense
  Missing depreciation and income tax. The yearly engine (loanCalculations.ts:432) correctly computes noi - interest - depreciation - tax. Overstates net income by ~$165K/yr on a $5M property.

  C2. Cash Flow Statement Fails ASC 230 Reconciliation

  financialEngine.ts:408-413
  operatingCashFlow + financingCashFlow does not equal the change in endingCash. The discrepancy equals cumulative depreciation. Every month produces an unreconciled cash flow statement.

  C3. Company Incentive Fee Can Go Negative

  financialEngine.ts:563
  incentiveFeeRevenue = totalPropertyGOP * global.incentiveManagementFee
  No Math.max(0, ...) clamp. Property-level (line 360) correctly clamps. During ramp-up with negative GOP, company revenue is understated.

  C4. Balance Sheet Excludes Land from Assets

  ConsolidatedBalanceSheet.tsx:92-93
  propertyBasis = purchasePrice * (1 - landPct) + buildingImprovements
  Land is non-depreciable but is still an asset. At 25% default, assets are understated by 25% of every purchase price.

  C5. Balance Sheet Structurally Cannot Balance (A != L+E)

  ConsolidatedBalanceSheet.tsx:165-168 & Dashboard.tsx:953-957
  Multiple structural causes: land excluded from assets (C4), pre-opening costs not tracked, refinance closing costs unrecorded. The displayed TOTAL ASSETS and TOTAL L&E will show different numbers.

  C6. Dashboard: Taxable Income Uses Accumulated Cross-Property Interest

  Dashboard.tsx:1180
  const taxableIncome = noi - totalInterest - depreciation
  totalInterest is a running sum across the forEach loop. Property 2 deducts Property 1's interest too. Systematically understates tax, overstates ATCF. ~$25K/yr error per additional property at typical rates.

  C7. Dashboard: Equity Applies LTV to Total Investment (Not Property Value)

  Dashboard.tsx:1126, 3100, 1209
  total += totalInvestment * (1 - ltv)
  Should be totalInvestment - (propertyValue * ltv). Pre-opening costs and reserves are 100% equity-funded by CRE convention. Understates equity by ~$112K per property (21% relative error), corrupting IRR and equity multiples.

  C8. Depreciation Applied Before Property Acquisition (Yearly Engine)

  loanCalculations.ts:422
  const depreciation = loan.annualDepreciation  // every year, regardless of acquisition
  acquisitionYear is computed but only gates capital expenditures, not depreciation. Overstates pre-acquisition depreciation by ~$75K cumulative for a Year 3 acquisition.

  C9. Interest Rate Ignores Property-Level Override (Monthly Engine)

  financialEngine.ts:254 vs loanCalculations.ts:117
  Monthly engine uses only global.debtAssumptions.interestRate. Yearly engine supports property.acquisitionInterestRate. A property at 6.5% vs global 9% = ~$147K/yr overstated debt service in the monthly pro forma. Same issue
  applies to acquisitionTermYears.

  C10. SAFE Notes Misclassified as Liabilities (Company Balance Sheet)

  Company.tsx:403-440
  SAFE notes are equity instruments (ASC 480), not debt. Overstates liabilities, understates equity, distorts leverage ratios. Equity section also missing Paid-In Capital.

  loanCalculations.ts:266 — yearsFromRefi = yearEnd - refi.refiYear + 1 returns nonzero for the year before refinance occurs when called with yearEnd = refiYear - 1. Currently masked by caller guard but latent defect.

  H5. Refinance Transition Year Drops Acquisition Debt Service

  loanCalculations.ts:304-321 — The refi year exclusively charges 12 months of refi payments, dropping any acquisition-loan months. Misstates debt service by the differential between acquisition and refi payments.

  H6. Dashboard toLoanParams Omits landValuePercent

  Dashboard.tsx:273-291 — Forces 25% default for all properties, ignoring property-specific land percentages. ~$10K/yr depreciation error per property.

  H7. Dashboard toGlobalLoanParams Omits salesCommissionRate

  Dashboard.tsx:267-271 — Falls back to legacy commissionRate or DEFAULT_COMMISSION_RATE. On a $10M exit, a 1% rate difference = $100K error.

  H8. Dashboard Balance Sheet Ignores Acquisition Date Offset

  Dashboard.tsx:919-927 — Amortization loop starts from month 0 regardless of acquisition date. Overstates principal paid, understates debt outstanding for staggered acquisitions.

  H9. || Used Instead of ?? for Numeric Fallbacks (30+ Locations)

  Dashboard.tsx:905, 913, 1125, 1151, etc. — A 0 value (e.g., 0% tax rate, 0% LTV for cash purchase) is silently replaced by the default. An explicit 0% tax rate becomes 25%.

  H10. Balance Sheet Acquisition Year Guard Off-by-One

  ConsolidatedBalanceSheet.tsx:87 — year <= acqYear should be year < acqYear. Properties acquired during a year are omitted from that year's entire balance sheet.

  H11. Balance Sheet Depreciation Not Prorated for Partial Years

  ConsolidatedBalanceSheet.tsx:112-114 & loanCalculations.ts:422 — Uses whole-year year - acqYear instead of actual months. Understates accumulated depreciation by up to 11 months in the acquisition year.

  H12. Company.tsx: 40+ Hardcoded length: 10 in JSX Tables

  Company.tsx:830, 838, 857, ... (48 occurrences) — Ignores projectionYears from global assumptions. Tables break or truncate if projection period != 10.
